The Density Formula

Density (ρ) = Mass (m) / Volume (V)

Where:

  • Density (ρ) is the mass per unit volume of a substance
  • Mass (m) is the amount of matter in an object
  • Volume (V) is the amount of space an object occupies

Common Density Values

Water: 1.0 g/cm³ or 1000 kg/m³
Iron: 7.87 g/cm³ or 7870 kg/m³
Aluminum: 2.70 g/cm³ or 2700 kg/m³
Air: 0.0012 g/cm³ or 1.2 kg/m³
Gold: 19.3 g/cm³ or 19300 kg/m³
