Understanding the Formulas

Density =

Mass / Volume

Mass =

Density × Volume

Volume =

Mass / Density

SI Units Cheat Sheet

Mass

SI Unit: Kilogram (kg)

Commonly Used: Gram (g), Milligram (mg)

Volume

SI Unit: Cubic Meter (m³)

Commonly Used: Liter (L), Milliliter (mL), Cubic Centimeter (cm³)

Note: 1 mL is the same as 1 cm³.

Density

SI Unit: Kilogram per Cubic Meter (kg/m³)

Commonly Used: Gram per Cubic Centimeter (g/cm³) or Gram per Milliliter (g/mL)
